Our AI model is trained to identify and prioritize the most salient information in a presentation. While accuracy is very high, the quality of the summary can depend on the clarity and structure of the original PPT. It's designed to be a powerful starting point for understanding, not a replacement for critical reading.

Currently, our summarizer focuses on the textual content within the presentation (titles, bullet points, notes, etc.). While we plan to incorporate image analysis (OCR) in future versions, the current model will not be able to summarize text embedded within images.

About This Project

This PPT Summarizer is a 5th Semester Computer Engineering project from Gujarat Technological University. Our mission was to apply modern Natural Language Processing techniques to solve a common problem for students and professionals alike: information overload. We aimed to build a tool that is not only functional but also secure, user-friendly, and professional.
